prompt: Standing silently in the quiet snowfall

Not exactly on prompt, but I've always wanted a 2d particle simulator. It's basically cellular automata with materials with various behaviors & physics. Draw on the canvas below to start.

drafted 03-09-2023 at RC creative coding meetup

I just wanted to play around with the cellular automata aspect, so I cribbed the canvas, UI, event management code, and sand behavior from jason.today's excellent writeup on making a falling sand pixel simulator.

what I added:

  • refactored the original code down to be much simpler/smaller
  • simple water simulation

bugs to fix:

  • water level should equalize between two tubes; need to simulate pressure
  • fix water jitter with the top row modulus remainder's worth of pixels
  • things can currently "fall" through an empty diagonal in an otherwise contiguous barrier
