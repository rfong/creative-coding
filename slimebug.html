Interesting / funny observations about this sketch:

  • To simplify the math, the bugs are drawn within a translated relative frame of reference. However, the slime trails need to be drawn in absolute coordinates.
  • A legitimate use case for "absolute butt coordinates"
  • The slime trails are attenuated without factoring in a time variable by using a stack of slime sizes that is decremented proportionally to the slime trail index.
