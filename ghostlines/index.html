ghost lines

Today's prompts: use lines wisely (I used them unwisely), self-portrait, look deep into the void. What I did:

  • sampled a webcam stream in greyscale
  • found the frame's center of "mass" (where black=hi, white=lo)
  • randomly sampled a subset of the points that changed values between sample frames by over a nontrivial threshold
  • drew vectors of the change in overall frame center of mass from those points

Draft is shamefully nonperformant, and this method of choosing lines doesn't always line up with intuition, but I had fun.
